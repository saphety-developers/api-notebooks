Services#

Send invoices using legal format
Use this service to send invoices in Portuguese legal format CIUS-PT.

Send PDF invoices by email
Use this service to sign, store and send PDF invoices. The PDF invoice will be sent by email to your costumer.

Store PDF invoices
Use this service to sign and store PDF invoices. The PDF invoice will be signed and stored, and not sent to your costumers.

Check invoice status in archive
Use this service to check an invoice status in archive.

Use this service to check an invoice status transitions in archive.

Use this service to get invoice formats from archive.
